FALLOUT NEW VEGAS WIKI

Origins: The NCR is a post-apocalyptic nation that emerged in the aftermath of the Great War, following the nuclear devastation that occurred in the Fallout universe. It was founded in the region that was once the state of California and parts of Nevada, with its origins tracing back to a group of settlers and Vault Dwellers who aimed to establish a democratic and organized government.

Government: The NCR is a republic, with a democratic government structure that includes a President and a Congress. It aims to emulate the pre-war democratic ideals of the United States, making it one of the few relatively stable and functional political entities in the post-apocalyptic wasteland.

Territory: The NCR's territory spans across much of the former California and parts of Nevada. The capital of the NCR is Shady Sands, which later becomes known as the New Vegas area.

Society: The NCR is characterized by a commitment to democratic values, the rule of law, and a general sense of order. They have a standing army known as the NCR Armed Forces and various governmental agencies to maintain control and provide security within their territory.

Conflict and Expansion: Throughout Fallout: New Vegas, the NCR is in direct competition and conflict with other factions, most notably Caesar's Legion, a brutal and autocratic empire. The conflict over control of the Mojave Wasteland serves as a central plot point in the game.

Issues: The NCR, despite its democratic ideals, is not without its problems. It faces challenges related to corruption, bureaucracy, and expansionism. In the Mojave Wasteland, they struggle to maintain control and deal with the harsh realities of the post-apocalyptic world.
