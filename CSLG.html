FALLOUT NEW VEGAS WIKI

Origins: Caesar's Legion is a brutal and autocratic empire that has emerged in the post-apocalyptic Mojave Wasteland. It is led by a charismatic and authoritarian figure known as Caesar, whose real name is Edward Sallow. Caesar's Legion originated from a group of Roman soldiers and scholars who, after the Great War, found themselves in the wasteland. Inspired by the ancient Roman Empire, Caesar's Legion adopted many aspects of Roman culture and governance.

Leadership: Caesar is the supreme leader of the Legion, often considered a god-like figure by his followers. Below him, the Legion is organized into a strict hierarchical structure, with Caesar's authority absolute. Caesar's most trusted subordinates are the Legates, high-ranking officers who lead the Legion's armies.

Territory: The Legion primarily operates in the Mojave Wasteland and aims to conquer and control the region. Their capital city, and the heart of their power, is Caesar's camp, located at Fortification Hill. The Legion is in direct conflict with the New California Republic (NCR) for control of the Mojave.

Conflict and Expansion: Caesar's Legion is expansionist, seeking to assimilate or conquer other tribes and communities in the wasteland. Their brutal methods and warlike nature make them a formidable and feared presence in the Mojave.

- **Symbolism: The Legion is heavily inspired by ancient Roman culture, including its military tactics, language (Latin is commonly spoken), and use of Roman iconography, such as the eagle and the bull.

Philosophy: Caesar's Legion believes in the harsh philosophy of "Hegelian Dialectic," where they believe that through the harsh process of conflict and conquest, they can create a stronger and more stable society.
